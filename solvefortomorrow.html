What's the Samsung Solve for Tomorrow Contest?

Did you see us? I was part of the team from Loudoun Valley High School in Purcellville, VA. We built an emergency alert system for remote trails.
For more information, watch the video below.

We were lucky enough to be chosen as one of the top 15 national finalists, which meant we pitched our project to a panel of judges at Samsung 837 in New York City. Check it out below...

We were fortunate to be named one of five national winners for our project and effort. We were awarded $120,000 in Samsung technology and $10,000 in Adobe software, which we used to create a STEM Lab at Loudoun Valley High School. This is now an open space to students to use to pursue their interests in STEM!
