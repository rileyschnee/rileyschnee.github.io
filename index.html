About Me

Riley Schnee

I am a Junior at the University of Michigan, studying Computer Science in the College of Engineering. This year, I am the Team Lead for the CLAWS project team, which works on augmented and virtual reality applications for human space exploration. In addition, I am an instructional aide for EECS 280: Programming and Introductory Data Structures. This past summer, I was a software engineering intern on Facebook's Marketplace platform.

My initial interest in programming evolved from my love of video games and desire to become a video game developer. In high school, I took two introductory programming classes, became involved in starting my school's STEM Club, and was a part of the LoudounCodes computer science initiative in my county. In my free time, I enjoy working on independent projects while listening to music, spending time with family and friends, reading fantasy and sci-fi novels, and playing video games with my dad and younger sister.
